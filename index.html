About ABC

ABC Public School & College is a co-educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Our experienced faculty, student-centric approach, and modern campus ensure that every learner receives the guidance and environment they need to grow.

Experienced Faculty

Our teachers are dedicated professionals who focus on conceptual clarity and individual attention to every student.

Holistic Development

We emphasize academics, values, sports, arts, and life skills to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Modern Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, laboratories, and a well-equipped library support an engaging and effective learning experience.

Academics

ABC offers a comprehensive academic structure from primary schooling to undergraduate college programs.

School Programs

  • Primary (Class 1–5) – Foundation building in all subjects.
  • Middle (Class 6–8) – Focus on concepts and skills.
  • Secondary (Class 9–10) – Board exam preparation.
  • Senior Secondary (Class 11–12) – Streams: Science, Commerce, Humanities.

College Programs

  • B.Sc. (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)
  • B.Com (General & Honors)
  • B.A. (Various specializations)
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

Admissions

Admissions at ABC are based on a transparent and merit-based process. Parents and students are encouraged to visit the campus and interact with our counsellors.

Admission Process

  1. Fill the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents at the admission office.
  3. Appear for entrance test or interaction (if applicable).
  4. Confirm admission through fee payment.
